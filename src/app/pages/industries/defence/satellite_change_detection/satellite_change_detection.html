Step-by-Step Methodology

Multi-Sensor Data Ingestion and Preprocessing

  • Automated daily ingestion of Sentinel-1 (SAR) and Sentinel-2 (Optical) data
  • Cloud-independent monitoring of strategic zones using radar-optical fusion
  • Unified spatial resampling and sub-pixel alignment of multi-spectral and radar bands

Advanced Semantic Segmentation

  • Simultaneous processing of multi-date stacks using custom CNN architectures
  • Combined analysis of optical and SAR signatures to identify structural damage
  • Generation of high-fidelity change masks that filter out environmental noise

Spatiotemporal Object Detection

  • Specialized deep learning nodes for identifying fortifications, vehicle deployments, and craters
  • Real-time visualization of the spatial distribution of changes across combat zones
  • Mapping the evolution of battlefield features through a time-series interface

Strategic Delivery and Reporting

  • Rapid processing of raw imagery into actionable intelligence
  • Research-ready change masks
  • Seamless export of intelligence products into military-grade GIS platforms
