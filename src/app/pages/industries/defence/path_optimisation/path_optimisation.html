Path Optimization/Navigation

Workflow Overview

LizoAI can generate optimal, energy- and time-efficient routes across complex terrain by fusing DEMs, satellite intelligence, and live environmental conditions.

[ Video of Workflow ]

Step-by-Step Methodology

Multi-Source Terrain & Environment Data Ingestion

  • High-resolution DEMs (LiDAR-derived, SRTM, TanDEM-X, UAV DEMs)
  • Optical and SAR satellite imagery for surface condition inference
  • Land cover, vegetation density, and surface roughness layers
  • Climate and weather feeds (rainfall, temperature, soil moisture proxies)
  • Optional vehicle / agent parameters (mass, power, fuel, mobility limits)

Terrain Normalisation & Feature Extraction

  • Slope, aspect, curvature, and elevation gradients derived from DEMs
  • Terrain roughness, obstacle density, and traversability indices
  • Soil and surface condition estimation using satellite + climate fusion
  • Wetness, flood susceptibility, and soft-ground likelihood modelling
  • Dynamic cost surfaces generated per terrain feature

Energy & Time Cost Modelling

  • Energy expenditure models based on slope, surface type, and load
  • Speed degradation models for rough, wet, or unstable terrain
  • Vehicle- or agent-specific mobility constraints applied dynamically
  • Separate but coupled energy cost and time cost layers
  • Confidence weighting for uncertain or rapidly changing conditions

Real-Time Environmental Intelligence Update

  • Live weather updates adjust soil strength and traction estimates
  • Satellite refreshes detect flooding, landslides, snow, or vegetation change
  • Temporal decay applied to stale data to avoid overconfidence
  • Rapid re-weighting of cost surfaces without full recomputation

Multi-Objective Path Optimisation

  • Optimisation balances: Minimum energy, Minimum time, Risk avoidance, and Terrain uncertainty
  • Start and end points defined manually or via GPS / mission feed
  • Supports hard constraints (e.g., must arrive within X time)
  • Supports soft constraints (e.g., prefer low energy but allow trade-offs)

Scenario Testing & What-If Analysis

  • Simulate worsening weather or soil saturation
  • Compare energy-optimal vs time-critical routes
  • Test vehicle or payload changes and visualise bottlenecks
  • Quantify cost deltas between routing strategies

Live Navigation & Re-Routing

  • Continuous position tracking of vehicle or agent with deviation detection
  • Automatic re-routing if terrain conditions degrade
  • Alerting when energy or time budgets are at risk
  • Confidence-aware navigation guidance

Delivery & Decision-Ready Outputs

  • Optimised routes as vector paths and navigation grids
  • Energy and time consumption estimates per route segment
  • Risk and confidence layers for situational awareness
  • Export to GIS, mission systems, or onboard autonomy stacks
