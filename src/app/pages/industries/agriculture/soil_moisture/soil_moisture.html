Forest Cover and Biomass Mapping

LizoAI quantifies ecosystem health and carbon sequestration potential by synthesizing multi-sensor satellite data into high-resolution biomass maps. By correlating Sentinel-1 structural data with optical vegetation indices, the platform provides precise estimates of forest density and carbon storage for climate research and environmental auditing.

Step-by-Step Methodology

Data Loading and Preprocessing

  • Multi-sensor acquisition of Sentinel-1 (SAR) and Sentinel-2 (Optical) data
  • Harmonization of Sentinel-1 backscatter with radiometrically & atmospherically corrected imagery
  • Cloud, shadow & artifact masking for clean temporal analysis
  • Pixel-level alignment of Sentinel-1 backscatter and spectral data layers

Biomass and Canopy Computation

  • Modeling the relationship between Sentinel-1 backscatter intensity and vertical tree structure
  • Integration of vegetation indices to capture leaf health and canopy density
  • Estimation of Above Ground Biomass (AGB) based on Sentinel-1 backscatter-to-height correlations
  • Calculation of carbon storage metrics across diverse forest classification

Spatiotemporal Mapping

  • Identification of long-term trends in forest degradation or secondary regrowth
  • Mapping of biomass density tiers to highlight high-carbon storage reservoirs
  • Detection of subtle canopy changes using multi-temporal fusion of Sentinel-1 and Sentinel-2 data
  • Automated segmentation of forest cover into density-based classes

Delivery and Interpretation

  • Export research-ready biomass density maps with metadata
  • Provision of verifiable datasets for carbon credit and offset auditing
  • Visualization of forest health alerts for conservation management
  • High-resolution outputs compatible with climate modeling frameworks
