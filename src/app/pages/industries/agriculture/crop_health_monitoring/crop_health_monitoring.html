Crop Health Monitoring

Workflow Overview

LizoAI optimizes agricultural productivity by transforming multi-temporal satellite data into actionable crop vigor assessments. By monitoring vegetation indices like NDVI over critical growing seasons, the platform identifies areas of stress and health variability to support data-driven irrigation and fertilizer management.

Step-by-Step Methodology

Data Acquisition & Preprocessing

  • Multi-spectral optical data (Sentinel-2 bands)
  • AOI-based bounding box & cloud-cover filtering
  • High-resolution resampling for pixel alignment
  • Atmospheric correction for multi-temporal consistency

Vegetation Index Computation

  • Multi-date sampling for time-series analysis (April–August)
  • NDVI calculation for vegetation health
  • Temporal snapshot integration (2023 vs 2024)

Spatio-Temporal Analysis

  • Seasonal growth pattern & anomaly detection
  • Month-wise and year-wise vigor comparison
  • Correlation with climate trends (rainfall, temperature)
  • Stress factor analysis (climate vs pest impact)

Decision Support & Delivery

  • Export research-ready figures
  • Layered visualization of NDVI health gradients
  • Actionable insights for yield prediction & stress mitigation
  • Detailed metadata and temporal logs for historical health auditing
