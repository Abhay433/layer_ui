Flood Induntation Modelling

Workflow Overview

LizoAI models, predicts, and visualises floodplain inundation in real time by combining terrain, hydrology, and live environmental forcing. Designed for riverine floods, urban flooding, emergency response, and infrastructure planning, the platform delivers high-fidelity situational awareness for mission-critical flood mitigation.

Step-by-Step Methodology

Terrain, Hydrology & Infrastructure Data Ingestion

  • High-resolution DEMs (LiDAR, photogrammetry, satellite)
  • River networks, catchment boundaries, and drainage systems
  • Land cover, impervious surfaces, and roughness coefficients
  • Infrastructure layers (levees, roads, buildings)
  • Real-time rainfall, river gauge, and climate forecasts aligned to a common hydrologic grid

Terrain Conditioning & Hydraulic Preparation

  • DEM hydrological correction and depression handling
  • River channel and floodplain separation
  • Levee and embankment enforcement
  • Flow direction and accumulation modelling
  • Resolution adaptation for urban vs rural zones

Rainfall–Runoff & Boundary Condition Modelling

  • Catchment-scale rainfall–runoff simulation
  • Soil moisture–dependent infiltration modelling
  • Dynamic boundary inflows from upstream gauges
  • Tide and storm surge coupling (for coastal floodplains)
  • Scenario-based forcing (design storms,forecasts,extremes)

Real-Time Inundation Simulation

  • 2D flow routing across floodplains
  • depth velocity and extent computation
  • Time-stepped propagation with adaptive resolution
  • Continuous updating as new rainfall or gauge data arrives
  • Uncertainty-aware simulation envelopes

Infrastructure Interaction & Failure Scenarios

  • Overtopping and breaching of levees
  • Road and culvert flow constrictions
  • Urban flow channelisation effects
  • Scenario testing for infrastructure failure
  • Impact of mitigation measures (levees, diversions)

Exposure, Impact & Emergency Intelligence

  • Identification of inundated assets and populations
  • Flood depth–damage estimation
  • Arrival-time mapping for evacuation routing
  • Critical infrastructure risk prioritisation
  • Confidence-weighted hazard zoning

Live Monitoring & Adaptive Re-Simulation

  • Continuous ingestion of live rainfall and river data
  • Automatic re-simulation as conditions change
  • Alert escalation based on defined thresholds
  • Scenario branching for worst-case planning

Delivery & Operational Outputs

  • Real-time inundation maps and animations
  • Depth,velocity and hazard rasters
  • Emergency-ready dashboards
  • GIS and system exports
  • Audit-ready modelling lineage and documentation
