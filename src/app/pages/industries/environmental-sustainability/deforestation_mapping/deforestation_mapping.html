Deforestation Mapping and Monitoring

LizoAI detects critical environmental shifts by converting multi-temporal satellite imagery into interpretable change maps. The platform identifies precise deforestation events and patterns over time, providing actionable intelligence for forest conservation and regulatory compliance.

Step-by-Step Methodology

Data Loading & Preprocessing

  • Open-source optical satellite data (e.g., Sentinel-2, Landsat)
  • Radiometric & atmospheric correction
  • Georeferencing & coregistration across dates
  • Cloud, shadow & water masking

Change Feature Computation

  • Spectral indices computation (NDVI, NBR)
  • Delta (difference) layers between time periods
  • Temporal summaries (mean, median, trend)

Spatiotemporal Mapping

  • Identify areas with significant vegetation change
  • Classify as deforested, degraded, or stable
  • Time-series visualisation & animated maps

Delivery & Interpretation

  • Export deforestation/change maps with metadata
  • Provide simple temporal control (play/pause, frame slider)
  • Outputs are research‑ready, interpretable maps
