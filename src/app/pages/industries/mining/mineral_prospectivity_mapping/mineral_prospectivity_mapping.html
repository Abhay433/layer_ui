Mineral Prospectivity Mapping

LizoAI streamlines mineral exploration by integrating multi-source data to generate predictive mineral prospectivity maps. By training advanced AI models on integrated geophysical, geological, and satellite datasets, the platform correlates hidden patterns with known deposits to identify high-potential target zones with measurable confidence.

Step-by-Step Methodology

Data Ingestion

  • Geophysical datasets (gravity, magnetic, derivatives)
  • Geological datasets (folds, faults, lineaments, age)
  • Satellite datasets (ASTER, SRTM, LANDSAT)
  • Known mineral occurrences (training positive samples)

Feature Generation & Sampling

  • Sample geospatial layers at locations of known deposits
  • Create contrasting unlabeled background samples
  • Label positive and negative classes
  • Merge into a training dataset for modeling
