Safety & Risk Monitoring

Protecting lives and assets through real-time slope stability and environmental hazard alerts.

Workflow Overview

Mining operations face constant risks from land subsidence, slope failures, and hazardous gas leaks. Our AI system monitors InSAR (Interferometric Synthetic Aperture Radar) data and IoT sensor feeds to detect millimeter-level ground movements. This acts as an early warning system to evacuate personnel before a disaster occurs.
