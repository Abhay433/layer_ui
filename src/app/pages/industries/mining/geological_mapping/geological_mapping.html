Geological Mapping

Workflow Overview

LizoAI transforms multi-source geospatial and geophysical data into high-resolution geological maps using AI-driven pattern recognition. By integrating satellite imagery with field observations, the platform delivers precise structural and lithological interpretations for rapid exploration and decision-making.

Step-by-Step Methodology

Multi-Source Data Loading

  • Satellite imagery
  • Field observations
  • Existing geological maps
  • Geophysical datasets (if available)

Preprocessing and Data Preparation

  • Data cleaning & alignment
  • Georeferencing & projection
  • Feature extraction (terrain, lithology, structures)

Modeling and Interpretation

  • AI-assisted pattern recognition (supervised & unsupervised ML models)
  • Integration with geophysical and geological data
  • Structural and stratigraphic interpretation
  • Iterative expert validation

Decision-Ready Outputs

  • High-resolution geological map
  • Layered map (lithology, structures, faults)
  • Metadata & confidence metrics
  • Downloadable/printable formats
