Operational Optimization

Enhancing mine productivity through spatial analytics and real-time haulage tracking.

Workflow Overview

This workflow focuses on the "Digital Twin" approach. We use high-resolution LiDAR and drone data to create a live 3D model of the mine site. This helps in optimizing truck routes, managing stockpile volumes, and reducing fuel consumption by identifying the steepest or most inefficient paths.

Summarised Workflow

  • 3D Terrain mapping using Drone-LiDAR.
  • Analysis of Haul Road geometry and rolling resistance.
  • AI-based dispatch system for truck-shovel synchronization.
  • Real-time monitoring of machinery health.
